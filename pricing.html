Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes, the free plan is completely free with no time limits. You can use it as long as you want with all the core features included.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade to Premium anytime to unlock advanced features, or downgrade back to Free if you no longer need premium features.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money and bank transfers for premium subscriptions. More payment options coming soon.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No credit card required. You can start using NkwaBiz immediately with the free plan. Only upgrade to Premium when you're ready.

What happens if I cancel my Premium subscription?

You'll automatically move back to the free plan. Your data remains safe and you keep access to all free features.

Is there a limit to how many products I can add?

No limits. Both Free and Premium plans allow unlimited products, so you can grow your business without worrying about restrictions.
